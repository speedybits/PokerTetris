How To Play - Basic Controls

Welcome to Cardtris! This card-based puzzle game combines Tetris mechanics with poker hands.

• Touch the left or right side of the screen to move cards

• Tap the Quick Drop button to instantly drop a card

• Create poker hands horizontally or vertically with 5 cards

• Watch the next card preview in the top-left corner

• Your level and score are shown in the top-right

Poker Hands & Base Scoring

Valid poker hands (from lowest to highest):

• One Pair: 25 points

• Two Pair: 50 points

• Three of a Kind: 100 points

• Straight: 150 points

• Flush: 200 points

• Full House: 300 points

• Four of a Kind: 500 points

• Straight Flush: 1000 points

• Royal Flush: 2000 points

Level System

Each level increases your score multiplier but removes lower poker hands:

• Level 1: All poker hands valid (1x points)

• Level 2: Pairs no longer count (2x points)

• Level 3: Two Pairs also removed (3x points)

• Level 4: Three of a Kind removed (4x points)

• Level 5: Straight removed (5x points)

• Level 6: Flush removed (6x points)

• Level 7: Full House removed (7x points)

• Level 8: Four of a Kind removed (8x points)

• Level 9+: Only Royal/Straight Flush (9x+ points)

Jokers Per Level

Jokers are wild cards that can represent any card. The number of Jokers in the deck increases with each level:

• Level 1: 2 Jokers

• Level 2: 4 Jokers

• Level 3: 6 Jokers

• Level 4: 8 Jokers

• Level 5: 10 Jokers

• Level 6: 12 Jokers

• Level 7: 14 Jokers

• Level 8: 16 Jokers

• Level 9+: 18 Jokers

Jokers are represented by a "?" symbol and can be used to complete any poker hand.
